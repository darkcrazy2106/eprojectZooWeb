Description !!!!

Giant Panda also known by most people as panda or panda is a species of bear native to China. The distinctive and unmistakable feature of pandas is that there are patches of black fur around the eyes, ears and 4 legs on a white background.

What do pandas eat ?

Although it is a carnivore, most of its nutrition comes from parts of bamboo and bamboo plants such as the trunk and bamboo shoots. In addition, in the wild, they sometimes eat a lot of other things such as weeds, birds or even carrion, in captivity they eat honey, fish eggs, bananas and special foods. different according to human calculation.

Where do pandas live ?

The main distribution of pandas in the wild is in central China, in Sichuan, Shaanxi and Gansu provinces, especially in Sichuan. Currently, there are only about 2000 pandas left in the world and are classified as "endangered" species. In the past, there was a time when there were less than 1,000 pandas in the world, it is the expansion of farming and deforestation that has caused the habitat of this animal to shrink.

Panda's Temperament !!!

The panda is a rather clumsy and forgetful animal.

Pandas usually live alone, each female has her own territory and there will be conflicts if another female bear invades their territory. Pandas are animals that communicate through sounds and sounds, and like many other animals, they also have a preference for marking their territory by scratching tree trunks or with urine.

Unlike many other bear species, pandas do not hibernate, but instead migrate to regions with warmer temperatures.

Male and female pandas only meet during the breeding season and after mating, the male pandas will leave. Pandas will lay a litter every two years.

Despite being a gentle animal, there have been recorded cases of pandas attacking humans, possibly out of anger.
